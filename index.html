METUP
🏆 Winner — ESIC × Demium Hackathon ESIC University 2022 AI · Metaverse · MarTech

METUP — AI-Powered Metaverse Marketing Platform

METUP is an AI-powered marketplace platform conceived and built during the ESIC × Demium Hackathon, where it secured 1st place. The core concept: as the metaverse grows into a major arena for brand presence and consumer experience, the marketing industry urgently needs specialist talent and infrastructure to navigate it effectively.

METUP bridges the gap — connecting freelance marketing professionals who specialise in metaverse environments (virtual worlds, immersive experiences, Web3 activations) with brands and companies looking to establish or strengthen their position in this emerging digital universe. An embedded AI matching engine pairs companies with the right creatives based on sector, budget, objective, and platform.

Key Contributions

  • Conceived the platform model and defined the two-sided marketplace value proposition (freelancers and brands).
  • Designed the AI-driven matching logic connecting brand objectives with specialist freelance profiles.
  • Built and presented the full business case — market sizing, revenue model, go-to-market strategy, and competitive positioning — to a jury of investors and startup founders at the Demium event.
  • Led the team from ideation to pitch within the compressed hackathon timeline.

SACRO — Premium Fast-Casual Kebab Concept

SACRO is a premium fast-casual kebab brand concept developed for Madrid as a Final Degree Project (TFG). The premise: reimagine one of the most popular but often undervalued street food formats — the kebab — through the lens of modern hospitality, premium ingredients, distinctive branding, and a refined dining environment.

The project challenged conventional assumptions about fast food, drawing inspiration from the success of premium burger and sushi concepts in Europe, and applying the same elevation strategy to the kebab category. SACRO is designed to occupy a distinct position between quick service and casual dining.

Key Contributions

  • Developed the full brand identity: name, visual language, tone of voice, and positioning strategy.
  • Conducted market analysis of the Madrid F&B landscape, competitive set, and target consumer profiling.
  • Built the business plan: financial projections, location strategy, operational model, and sourcing.
  • Designed the customer experience from pre-visit awareness through to in-store environment and post-visit retention.

Circular Hub
Entrepreneurial Project · ESIC 2025 Circular Economy · B2B

Circular Hub — Industrial Waste Marketplace

Circular Hub is an academic entrepreneurial project conceived at ESIC that addresses one of the most pressing inefficiencies in industrial supply chains: the management of surplus materials and industrial waste. The platform connects waste-generating companies (suppliers) with businesses that can repurpose those materials (buyers) through a digital marketplace.

By combining data-driven matching algorithms, logistics integration, and a streamlined B2B interface, Circular Hub aims to turn waste from a cost centre into a revenue opportunity — supporting the transition towards a circular economy in the industrial sector.

Key Contributions

  • Developed the product concept and defined the platform's core value proposition for both buyer and supplier sides.
  • Designed the marketplace matching model and logistics integration framework.
  • Researched the regulatory landscape, sustainability certifications, and circular economy frameworks relevant to the B2B industrial sector in Spain and Europe.
  • Built the business case including market sizing, revenue model, and scalability strategy.
